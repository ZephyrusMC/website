Who we are?

We are a lifesteal Minecraft server with the added ability to steal hearts from other players.

Zephyrus SMP

ZephyrusSMP is a one of kind, unique SMP that allows players to steal hearts from each other. Once a player kills another, they take a heart from them. If you die to a player, you lose a heart to them. Once you're out of hearts, you're banned for the rest of the day. With no claims and no rules on griefing.
